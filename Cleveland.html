Cleveland

Image Credit: (Skyline Scenes, n.d.)

History

Cleveland was incorporated in 1836 (World Population Review, n.d.)

Region

Cleveland is located in the Northeast region of Ohio (Ohio.org, n.d.)

Classification

Cleveland is classified as urban (Wikipedia, n.d.)

Population

The population of Cleveland is 359,606 (World Population Review, n.d.)

Average Income

The average income of $55,028 (World Population Review, n.d.), compared to the average household income in Ohio of $90,958 (Point2, n.d.).
