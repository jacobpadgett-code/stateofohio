Cincinnati

Image Credit: (Skyline Scenes, n.d.)

History

Cincinnati was incorporated in 1819 (World Population Review, n.d.)

Region

Cincinnati is located in the Southwest region of Ohio (Ohio.org, n.d.)

Classification

Cincinnati is classified as urban (Wikipedia, n.d.)

Population

The population of Cincinnati is 311,595 (World Population Review, n.d.)

Average Income

The average income of Cincinnati is $79,960 (World Population Review, n.d.), compared to the average household income in Ohio of $90,958 (Point2, n.d.).
